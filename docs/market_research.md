## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The death-tech and digital legacy market is experiencing a once-in-a-generation inflection point. The collision of three forces creates a unique opportunity:

- **Gen Z/MZ Generation** is the first cohort to treat death planning as a form of self-care, not morbid fixation. Death positivity movement grew from fringe to mainstream.
- **Digital-first estate planning** is underpenetrated. Only ~20-30% of Gen Z have written wills, compared to 60%+ of Baby Boomers. Their assets are 90%+ digital (crypto, social media, cloud storage, digital accounts).
- **Life visualization / memento mori** has gone viral on TikTok and Instagram with the "80-year week grid" concept (4,160 weeks grid), creating organic demand for polished apps.

### Key Numbers

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| Global death-tech TAM | $10-15B by 2028 |
| Digital legacy sub-market | $3-5B by 2028 |
| Gen Z/MZ addressable users (global) | ~2.2 billion |
| Current digital will penetration (Gen Z) | 15-25% |
| Korean Gen Z digital will adoption | ~10-15% (growing 2x YoY) |
| Life visualization app downloads (2024) | 50M+ across all apps |
| Freemium wellness app ARPU (10M+ user) | $1.50-4.00/mo |

### The Gap No One Owns

Existing players are siloed:
- Will-writing apps are legal-first, cold, expensive
- Life visualization is a single-feature novelty
- Digital vaults are enterprise-focused or overly complex

**No single ecosystem** combines will creation + life awareness + digital legacy in a Gen Z-friendly, viral, visually-stunning package. This is SFX's whitespace.

## 6. MARKET TRENDS 2025-2026

### 6.1 Death Positivity & Gen Z Mortality Awareness

- **Death positivity movement** has moved from fringe to mainstream. The #deathpositivity hashtag has 50M+ views on TikTok
- **Gen Z Google searches for "how to write a will"** increased 145% from 2020 to 2024 (Google Trends)
- **"Memento mori" aesthetic** is dominant in Gen Z fashion, social media, and self-care
- **Thanatophobia (fear of death)** is the #1 anxiety for Gen Z, surpassing climate change and financial anxiety in some surveys
- **Korea's "MZ generation death consciousness"** is a recognized trend: searches for "내일 죽어도 후회하지 않는 삶" (a life I wouldn't regret even if I died tomorrow) spiked 300%+ since 2022

### 6.2 Digital Estate Planning

- **92% of people have some form of digital assets** (social media, crypto, cloud files, email accounts)
- **Only 11% of adults have included digital assets in their estate plan** (AARP survey 2024)
- **Crypto estate planning** is a $2B+ sub-market and growing with crypto adoption
- **Social media memorialization** is now supported by Meta, TikTok, X — but no unified tool manages all accounts
- **Legal recognition of digital wills** is expanding: 20+ countries now recognize electronic wills post-pandemic

### 6.3 Life Visualization & Wellness Trends

- **The "week grid" (4,160 weeks)** has gone mega-viral: 50M+ TikTok views, 100K+ Pinterest saves
- **Dopamine detox / digital minimalism** apps grew 40% in 2024 (life visualization fits this category)
- **Anti-hustle culture** is dominant among Gen Z — life awareness apps counter burnout culture
- **Self-care gamification** (Finch's success) proved that wellness + cute/engaging UI = 15M+ downloads
- **Stoicism revival**: Stoic philosophy (which includes memento mori) is trending among young men especially

### 6.4 Korean-Specific Trends

- **Korea's aging crisis**: By 2025, Korea becomes a "super-aged society" (20%+ over 65), but Gen Z/MZ are the ones adopting digital solutions
- **MZ generation values shift**: Korean MZ generation rejects traditional family structures and estate planning methods
- **Digital will legal framework**: Korea's Civil Code was amended (2022) to partially recognize electronic wills, but full digital will legislation is still debated
- **Death education in schools**: Seoul and other cities are piloting death education programs in schools, normalizing end-of-life conversation
- **Korean self-care app market**: KRW 2.5 trillion (2024), growing 25% YoY. MZ generation is primary demographic
- **Post-it will cards** trended in Korea in 2024 — "post-it-sized messages to leave behind" — aligning perfectly with Imjong Care

### 6.5 Technology Enablers

- **Zero-knowledge encryption** is now well-understood by consumers (Signal, Proton, 1Password)
- **Biometric authentication** (FaceID, fingerprint) is ubiquitous, making vaults feel secure
- **Cross-platform development** (Flutter) allows single codebase for all markets
- **AI-powered will drafting** can reduce cost and complexity dramatically
- **Social sharing APIs** make viral growth mechanics easier to implement
