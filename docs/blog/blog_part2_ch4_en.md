### Beyond Life: Confronting Our Digital Legacy After Death

The greatest defining characteristic of humanity is our 'finitude.' We will all face death, and this inescapable truth is what makes our lives beautiful and urgent. This is why I have established the philosophy of "Living Well = Dying Well" as the highest value of my projects.

However, in modern society, we are tragically unprepared for death in the digital realm. When we pass away, what happens to the tens of thousands of photos locked in our smartphones, the diaries scattered across clouds, and our most intimate thoughts? Most will be permanently locked behind forgotten passwords or coldly wiped from servers according to a platform's terms of service. 

The moment our physical existence "logs out," our digital legacy is reduced to junk data.

### A Time Capsule of Authenticity: The Legacy Vault

The 'Legacy Vault' is not a simple backup folder. It is the **ultimate time capsule**, strictly verified and cryptographically secured by my independent intelligence ecosystem, 'Origin'.

Inside this Vault resides my most honest voice recordings, raw contemplations that I would never share casually (the essence of `Memento Mori`), and the 'Intellectual Fingerprint' proven and curated by Origin. 

While I am alive, this data remains completely inaccessible to the outside world. However, the moment my physical death is verified (through a smart contract or a cryptographic key held by a designated heir), the Vault is unlocked. It becomes a final letter and a "Soul's Log" delivered intact to my family and loved ones, representing the truest version of myself.

It is 100% pure human originality, entirely free from corporate censorship or synthetic data corruption. This is the essence of the Legacy Vault.

### I Am a 'Legacy Architect' of the AI Era

Countless Big Tech companies and tech philosophers in Silicon Valley are racing to answer questions like, "How can machines perfectly replace humans?" or "How can we upload human brains into machines?"

I have chosen to walk the opposite path. I do not want machines to replace humans. Instead, I want to leverage overwhelming AI technology to permanently preserve the unique, irreplaceable 'Authenticity' that machines can never simulate.

I have resolved to move beyond being just a developer writing apps. In this digital age, I aim to be a **'Legacy Architect,'** a defender of human dignity.
